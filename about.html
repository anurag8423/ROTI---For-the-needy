WE BELIEVE IN A WORLD WITHOUT HUNGER

What is ROTI?

ROTI is a visionary website dedicated to eradicating hunger. It stands for "Reaching Out To Individuals." Our platform revolutionizes the way we combat food wastage, connecting those with surplus food from events with local NGOs, creating a network where no one goes hungry.

What we do?

At ROTI, we transform excess event food into a lifeline for the hungry. Event hosts connect with us, and we orchestrate seamless collaboration with nearby NGOs. This innovative process ensures surplus food becomes a source of sustenance for those in need, fostering a community united against hunger.
