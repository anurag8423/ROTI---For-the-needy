THE PROBLEM

One in ten people in India goes to bed hungry at night.

One in four children under age five is severely malnourished. But despite agricultural self-sufficiency, a steadily increasing GDP, and increased per capita consumption, India finds itself engulfed in a serious hunger crisis.

This is discernible by the continuous downslide on the Global Hunger Index (GHI). India ranked 107th out of 121 countries in 2022.
